This is the How-To page. A quick description of how to use the app and how bowling works.

Bowling is simple. You have two tries per frame to get all the pins at the end of the lane. The more pins you get the higher the score you are going to receive. The only way to get the highest score is to get strikes each frame.
A strike is using your first roll and getting all 10 pins. After your first throw if you get the rest of the standing pins then you get a spare which is also a good way to increase your score.
